TRUST AND OBEY

Chorus:
Trust and obey!
For there's no other way
To be happy with Jesus,
But to trust and obey.


2.

Can abide while we trust and obey.

3.

But our toil he doth richly repay;

Or we'll walk by his side in the way
What he says we will do, where he sends we will go;
Never fear, only trust and obey.

tis thy child returns to thee

Let me not implore in vain;

33. WE GIVE IMMORTAL PRAISE




WE GIVE IMMORTAL PRAISE

1.

We give immortal the praise to God the fathers love
Fall or our comforts love and better hope above
He sent his own eternal son
To die for the sin man has done

2.

To God the son belongs immortal glory too
Who brought us with man done from everlasting woe
And now he lives, and now he reigns
And see the fruit of all his pains.

3.

To God spirits name immortal worship give
Whose new- creating power makes the dead sinner live
His work completes the great design
And fills the soul with joy divine.

4.

Almighty God to thee be endless honour
The undivided three and the mysterious one,
Where reason fails, with all her pow'r
There faith prevail and love adores.



34. WHAT A FRIEND WE HAVE IN JESUS




WHAT A FRIEND WE HAVE IN JESUS

1.

What a friend we have in Jesus,
All our sins and griefs to bear!
What a privilege to carry
Everything to God in prayer!
O what a peace we often forfeit
O what needless pain we bear
All because we do not carry
Everything to God to prayer

2.

He we trials and temptations?
Is there trouble anywhere?
We should never be discouraged,
Take it to the lord in prayer.
Can we find a friend so faithful
Who will all our sorrows share?
Jesus knows every weakness,
Take it to the lord in prayer.

3.

Are we weak and heavy laden,
Cumbered with a load of care?
Precious savior, still our refuge,-
Take it to the lord in prayer.
Do thy friends despise, forsake thee?
Take it to the lord in prayer;
In his arms he'll take and shield thee,
You will find a solace there.
